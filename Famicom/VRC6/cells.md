# Cells

## Logic Cell

![logic_cell.jpg](imgstore/logic_cell.jpg)

- N-Pockets (who cares)
- P always left
- mirror symmetry (can be mirrored without swapping N/P)
- 16 stripes (m1 channels)
- dual-rails can be inside cell (see dff)
- configurable (see and3 for example)
